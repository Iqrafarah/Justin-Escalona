Justin  Escalona (1996)

Justin Escalona is a 25 year old artist from Chicago, IL. He moved to Los Angeles to study Film & TV Production at the prestigious University of Southern California. Four years ago during his freshman year of college, Justin founded 1340 COLLECTIVE. The brand is a lifestyle streetwear brand that has been seen on people like David Dobrik, Khalid, & Arizona Zervas. It is currently valued at over $5 million.
